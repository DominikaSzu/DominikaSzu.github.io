Hi there! I’m Dominika and I graduated with a master degree in Italian philology in Cracow.In June of 2017, I came to a meet-up of Geek Girls Carrots and I found out that front-end could be a right direction for me. Since then, I’ve been dedicating my time to learning front-end technologies . Learning on your own is fun, but I am looking for a place where I can learn much more and start my career as a junior front-end developer.

What happened so far?

After graduating, I worked in several projects in different corporations. I learned a lot there, but at some point I realized, I need a possibility to develop in my roles and learn new stuff. This is exactly what coding is about: there is always some new technology/something to learn/practice and that’s great .

What now?

In the meantime, I received a 6 months scholarship and a certificate of Front-End Web Development Nanodegree organized by Udacity in collaboration with Google. Aside from learning, I enjoy engaging with the community.

I'm moderating the group "Programuj, dziewczyno!" with almost 17 thousands of girls interested in the IT world. In June of 2018 together with other administrators, we have organized a meet-up in Warsaw for people who would want to find their place in IT.

As I'm a IT newbie myself, I also try to create learning opportunities to other newbies. I'm a part of a Girls.js which organizes JavaScript free fundamentals workshop for women. In 2018 we have organized two events. We grow fast: in May we gave an opportunity to learn to 9 girls, and in October to 33 participants (here you can find a post about the event that took place in May and here about the one from October).
